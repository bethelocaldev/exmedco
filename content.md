- **Layout:** Strict 12-column grid, generous whitespace, left-aligned content blocks, horizontal rule dividers between sections
- **Imagery Style:** Desaturated or navy-tinted photography; no stock photo clichés; prefer macro product shots, clean facility photography, and data visualisation graphics
- **Motion:** Subtle fade-in on scroll; no page-load animations; hover states with 200ms transitions; stat counters animate on viewport entry
- **Iconography:** Outlined, single-weight icons (Phosphor or Lucide style); no filled colourful icons
- **Button Style:** Rectangular with 2px solid border; primary = navy fill, white text; secondary = white fill, navy border & text
- **Card Style:** White background, 1px `#D9E3EF` border, 0 border-radius (or max 4px), subtle box-shadow on hover only
